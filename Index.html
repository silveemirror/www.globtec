Return Policy


1. Overview

At GLOBTECH Gadgets, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you are not completely happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return process. This Return Policy outlines the terms and conditions for returning products purchased from our online store.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, please make sure to:


a. Request a Return: Contact our customer support team within 10 business working days of receiving your order to initiate the return process. You can reach us via email, live chat, or phone.

b. Return Condition: Ensure that the product is in its original condition, unused, with all accessories, packaging, and labels intact. We reserve the right to deny a return if the product is not in its original condition.

c. Proof of Purchase: Provide proof of purchase, such as an order confirmation email or a receipt.

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:


a. Contact Customer Support: Reach out to our customer support team to request a return. We will provide instructions and a Return Authorization Number (RAN) if applicable.

b. Pack the Product: Pack the product securely, including all original accessories and packaging.

c. Include RAN (if provided): If you receive a Return Authorization Number, please include it with your return package.

d. Shipping: Ship the product to the address provided by our customer support team. You are responsible for the cost of return shipping.

4. Refunds and Processing

Once we receive your returned product and confirm that it meets the eligibility criteria, we will process your refund. The refund will be issued in the same form of payment used for the original purchase, unless otherwise agreed upon. Please allow up to 24 hours for the refund to appear in your account, as processing times may vary depending on your payment method and financial institution.

5. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a product that is damaged or defective, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will provide instructions on how to proceed, which may include a return, exchange, or repair, depending on the specific situation.


Make sure to keep your return policy up to date and in compliance with relevant laws and regulations.
